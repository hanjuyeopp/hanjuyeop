melissa y/project 
            The Y/PROJECT x Melissa shoe collaboration is a nod to Victorian vases,
            Profusely adorned and embellished, merged with the popular British tradition of porcelain vases shaped
            as miniature court shoes, distorting the idea of preciosity. The design ethos of Y/PROJECT has always been about the
            blending and bending of historical references and pop culture, of past and present, of high and low.
            This eco-friendly collaboration reflects a girl’s dream to wear a crystal shoe,
            but with all the comfort Melissa is renowned for.
